If you have been prompted to click an OK dialog asking if you want to add information to your registry, please click on the "Yes" button.


You should then get a message to indicate the addition was successful.


will have then applied changes to the following registry entry:

[HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Java VM]


You will need to restart your browser to resume where you left off.


If however, the message indicated that the addition was not successful, please contact your System Administrator for assistance in applying the fix.
